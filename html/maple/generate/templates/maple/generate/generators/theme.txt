Usage: nc [options] theme <themeName> [themeType]
-------------------------------------------------------
  themeName:required (hoge or hoge_hoge)
  themeType:default="normal"
                    simple | normal | full
  -----------------------------------------
  theme.ini            * 	   * 	   *
  icon_color.ini       * 	   * 	   *
  block_custom.ini     - 	   - 	   *
  page_custom.ini      - 	   - 	   *
  page_style.css       - 	   * 	   *
  style.css            * 	   * 	   *
  images               * 	   * 	   *
  language             * 	   * 	   *
  templates            * 	   * 	   *
  ------------------------------------------

Examples:

"nc theme hoge"
"nc theme hoge_default full"
"nc theme hoge_gray simple"
"nc theme hoge_red full"
