Usage: nc [options] action <action_name> [template_type]

Examples:

"action foo_view_main" generates:
 - MODULE_DIR/foo/view/main/Main.class.php (an Action class)
 - MODULE_DIR/foo/view/main/maple.ini (a configuration file)
 
"action foo_view_main simple" generates:
 - MODULE_DIR/foo/view/main/Main.class.php (an Action class)

Type "nc help" to list the general options.
